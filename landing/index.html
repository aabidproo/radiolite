Questions? Handled.

Is Radiolite really free?

Yes, Radiolite is 100% free and open-source. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or intrusive advertisements in the app.

How do I fix the "unverified developer" warning on Mac?

On macOS, simply right-click the app in your Applications folder and select "Open". This is a one-time requirement for apps not downloaded from the App Store. Alternatively, you can use the terminal command found in the Help section.

Can I listen to Arabic radio stations?

Absolutely! Radiolite features one of the most comprehensive lists of Arabic and MENA radio stations, including Holy Quran Radio, Al Jazeera, and local FM stations from Egypt, UAE, and Saudi Arabia.

Does it support keyboard shortcuts?

Yes, you can control playback and volume using global media keys or custom shortcuts defined within the app settings. This allows you to stay focused on your work while managing your radio.
